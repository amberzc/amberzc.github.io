About OcuNeurologix

OcuNeurologix is at the forefront of neurodiagnostic innovation. Our primary product, the Ocuscope, is a cutting-edge eye-tracking device designed to provide accurate, real-time assessments of neurological function, particularly for concussion evaluation.

The Ocuscope analyzes key eye movement metrics such as:

  • Pupil diameter and light reflex responses
  • Gaze trajectory and smooth pursuit gain
  • Saccadic speed, latency, and accuracy
  • Vestibulo-ocular reflex (VOR) and near point convergence (NPC)
  • Reaction time and inter-eye asymmetry

With data-driven insights and a focus on reliability, the Ocuscope supports clinicians and athletic trainers in making informed decisions about neurological health. Our goal is to make neuro-assessment tools more accessible and effective, empowering better outcomes for patients and athletes alike.

Our Vision

To revolutionize neurological diagnostics with smart, user-friendly technology that brings laboratory-grade accuracy to the field.
