About OcuNeurologix

OcuNeurologix is an innovative biomedical company focused on transforming concussion screening and neurological health using accessible, vision-based technology.

Our Mission

To empower coaches, trainers, athletes, and healthcare providers with intuitive and objective eye-tracking tools for early detection and management of brain injuries.

Why Ocuscope?

  • Portable and user-friendly diagnostic tool
  • On the field real-time analysis
  • Low cost design

Our Vision

We aim to revolutionize sports medicine by integrating technology with neurophysiology to safeguard cognitive health on and off the field.
